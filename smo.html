Social Media Optimization (SMO)

Social media optimization allows the owner of a website to drive traffic to their page and create leads for their business. Current estimates put the size of the internet at over a billion web pages. Your topic may easily have hundreds, thousands, or even tens of thousands of pages on it. In such a massive sea of content, how do we stand out among the crowd?

SEO is the singular most powerful tool that webmasters have to make sure that their sites get seen. Social media optimization (SMO) is a key component of that. Any social media platform will allow users to share your content. This is a great way to promote growth. As web designers, we will be greatly helped, and so will the user, if we allow our pages to be easily shareable. 50% of companies that aren’t succeeding in their SEO don’t utilize SMO.

SMO takes work, and it isn’t free (this is a common misconception). To drive a proper SMO campaign, you will need market research, keyword research, and time. It also takes a lot of trial and error to get the correct campaign for your business needs.

All these drive engagement, which is exactly what the savvy modern marketer is looking for. We want people not just visiting our sites, but buying the products we sell, sharing our content, and reading our articles. This is, after all, the ultimate point of creating a website in the first place. With the usage of social media, both we and the visitor are saying, “Hey, this is interesting! Come and join us over here!”

For this reason, many businesses utilizing a good SMO plan will interact with clients by liking their posts, making conversation, and creating fun and entertaining content. Doing so helps potential customers engage with a brand in more familiar and comfortable ways, and helps to drive traffic to a webpage.
